Introduce

Dancer Flow provides scoring service for dancers of all levels by measuring motion similarity in real time. Players can see how much their movements accurate to professional dancer.

Our open source vision model use for estimating poses of body parts such as head, shoulders, elbows, and hands. Then, we compare the video to your pose using PoseNet to determine how accurate their movements or pose was.
